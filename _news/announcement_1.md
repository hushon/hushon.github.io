We will present a new publication on machine unlearning at [ICCV 2023](https://iccv2023.thecvf.com/). We have explored a novel transfer learning scheme, named *Disposible Transfer Learning*, which scrubs the unimportant portion of the upstream task knowledge when conducting transfer learning. Our goal is to facilitate open-sourcing expert models by providing means to avoid the risk of exposing foundation models.
